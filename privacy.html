OceanBite

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

01The Short Version

OceanBite stores the minimum it needs to run a game: your Discord user ID, your Discord username, and your gameplay data. It does not collect your messages, email address, real name, or any other personal information — and it never sells or shares data with anyone.

02Data We Collect

When you interact with OceanBite (slash commands, buttons, menus, or modals), we store:

  • Your Discord user ID — a numeric identifier assigned by Discord, used as your save file's key;
  • Your Discord username — the public handle attached to your interactions, used solely for game purposes: showing names in clan member lists, tournaments, and leaderboards;
  • Server (guild) IDs of servers where you've used the Bot — used only for per-server leaderboards and tournaments;
  • Gameplay data — your in-game progress: levels, currencies, inventory, equipment, skills, quests, clan membership, pets, badges, statistics, and similar game state;
  • User-provided game content — clan names you create and game preferences you set (e.g. embed color).

We do not read or store message content, avatars, email addresses, IP addresses, or any data outside the game interactions listed above. Avatars are rendered live by Discord and never stored by us.

03How We Use Data

  • To operate the game: saving and loading your progress;
  • To provide social features: leaderboards, clans, and tournaments;
  • To keep the game fair: enforcing cooldowns and fair-play rules.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics resale, or any purpose unrelated to running the game.

04Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your data with third parties. The only parties that process your data are:

  • Discord, which transmits your interactions to the Bot (governed by Discord's Privacy Policy);
  • The hosting provider the Bot runs on, where the game database is stored.

Some gameplay data is visible to other players by design — for example, leaderboard rankings, clan membership, and your profile when another player views it.

05Data Retention & Deletion

Gameplay data is retained for as long as the Bot operates so your progress persists across servers and sessions.

You can delete all of your data yourself, instantly — no request needed:

  • In Discord, run /settings (or open /menu and pick ⚙️ Settings);
  • Press 🗑️ Delete my data and confirm.

Deletion takes effect immediately and removes everything we store about you. It is permanent: your game progress cannot be restored. If you run into any trouble, contact us (see below) and we will delete your data for you within 30 days.

06Security

Game data is stored in a private database accessible only to the Bot's operator. It is not exposed publicly, and access credentials are kept confidential. No method of storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures appropriate to the nature of the data (a game save file).

07Children

OceanBite is intended for users who meet Discord's minimum age requirement (13, or higher where local law requires). We do not knowingly collect data from children below that age; if you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it.

08Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be reflected on this page; continued use of the Bot after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.

09Contact

For privacy questions or help with data deletion, contact the operator on Discord: 0krypt.
