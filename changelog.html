🌊 v1.4.1 — Unlock alerts everywhere July 1, 2026

🐛 Fixes

  • Level-up unlock alerts now fire no matter how you leveled up — filling a bounty, claiming quests, or landing a voyage boss will notify you the same way casting does.

🌊 v1.4.0 — Ready-to-collect alerts July 1, 2026

🆕 New

  • You'll now get a message in your private thread when a cooked dish is ready to collect, or when your voyage's next leg (or the boss fight!) has arrived.

🌊 v1.3.0 — Level-up unlock alerts July 1, 2026

🆕 New

  • Leveling up now sends a message in your private fishing thread listing what just unlocked — new rods and boats to buy, new biomes to explore, or features like exotic fish, charms, and prestige.

🌊 v1.2.1 — Cleaner chest loot June 30, 2026

⚙️ Improved

  • Chest rewards on your catch card now show XP with a "+" (e.g. "+616 XP"), so it no longer blends into the coins amount next to it.

🌊 v1.2.0 — Dive again June 30, 2026

⚙️ Improved

  • Deep Dive result cards (surfaced or busted) now have a "Dive again" button, so you can start a new dive straight from the result instead of reopening the menu.

🌊 v1.1.0 — Changelog launch June 30, 2026

🆕 New

  • OceanBite now has a changelog! Update notes post in the community announcements channel, are browsable with /changelog, and live on the website.

⚙️ Improved

  • Casting reveals a one-time “Check line” clue during gamble cues, and only shows the button when it can actually help.
