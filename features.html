🎣 Fishing Core

Fishing is the heart of OceanBite. Cast your line, wait for the bite, reel in your catch. An interactive cast-wait-reel flow with patience cues, gamble windows, and anti-cheat verification keeps every cast engaging.

🐟
/fish — Cast your line

The main gameplay loop. Cast into your current biome, watch for procedural cues, and reel in at the right moment. Catches are influenced by your rod, bait, boat, pet, weather, buffs, and biome. Shinies, event fish, and relics can appear on any cast.

💰
/sell — Sell your haul

Turn your catch into money. Sell everything at once or pick what to keep. Prices scale with fish rarity and your Haggling skill.

💵
/balance — Check your wallet

View your current money and Sea Crystal balances.

🌤️
/weather — Fishing conditions

Weather rotates every 2 hours per biome — clear skies, rain (+30% rare chance), sunny spells (+25% money), fog, storms, and more. Check conditions before you cast.

📖
/fishdex — Collection log

Track every species you've caught, your personal records, and which shinies you've found. A completionist's dream.

🌊 Adventures Adventure

Beyond the basic cast there are multi-step expeditions, push-your-luck dives, cooperative boss fights, delivery contracts, and a full cooking system.

⚓
/voyage — Multi-leg expeditions

Embark on a real-time expedition with travel legs, interactive encounters, and a climactic boss-fish fight at the end. Loot accumulates along the way — surface to collect, or lose it all if you fall.

🤿
/dive — Deep Dive

Pay an entry fee and descend level by level. Each depth multiplies your haul but raises the bust chance (up to 85%). Surface to bank your winnings, or push deeper and risk it all. Exotic crates appear at depths 5, 10, and 15.

🍳
/galley — Cook dishes & buffs

Turn your catch into ingredient dishes with timed buffs. Learn recipes, cook meals, and apply galley buffs that stack with your other bonuses. Some recipes are unlocked through Community Goals and special achievements.

📋
/bounties — Harbor bounty board

Accept delivery contracts for fish and dishes. Bounties are tailored to what you can actually produce — only species from biomes you've unlocked and recipes you've learned will appear. Fill them for money, XP, Harbor Tribute, and rank.

👹
/boss — World Boss

A 48-hour cooperative fight that runs every two weeks (Wednesday–Friday UTC). Every cast while the boss is up deals damage equal to your catch weight. Everyone who lands 10+ casts gets a proportional share of the reward pot — recruiting friends always helps.

🎪
/event — Weekend & seasonal events

Every Saturday and Sunday features a rotating weekend event with unique fish, a bonus shop, and special mechanics. Seasonal events (holidays, etc.) run for longer stretches with exclusive rewards and event-only pets.

🏆
/tournament — Competitions

Three layers of competition running simultaneously:

  • /tournament start — Server tournaments: an admin kicks one off, and the heaviest haul by the deadline wins the pot.
  • /tournament global — Global daily & weekly tournaments where every cast contributes weight automatically. Top 10 win prizes.
  • Server Showdown — A weekly server-vs-server competition. Your server's total weight divided by the square root of its participants is the score, so small servers can genuinely compete. Winners get +5% money & XP for the next week.

🛒 Gear & Shop Core

Upgrade your loadout to catch rarer fish, earn more money, and unlock new abilities.

🏪
/shop — Buy equipment

Browse and purchase rods, boats, bait, charms, and pets. Each tier unlocks better stats and new mechanics.

🎣
/rod — View & equip rods

Each rod has a rarity multiplier and special perks. Rods develop affinity with biomes the more you use them there.

🚤
/boat — View & equip boats

Boats boost money, XP, and fish weight. Better boats also reduce cooldown between casts.

🪱
/bait — Check & switch bait

Bait is consumed on each cast and boosts specific stats — rare chance, money, XP, or quantity. Stock up at the shop.

✨
/charms — Equip charms

Unlocked at level 20. Charms provide passive bonuses — stack up to your charm slots for a custom loadout.

🐾
/pets — Active pet

Pick a companion that tags along on every cast. Pets grant unique bonuses and some are exclusive to seasonal events or achievements.

🗺️
/biome — Travel to new waters

Seven biomes to explore, from the starting Ocean all the way to the Arctic Abyss (level 95). Each biome has unique fish species, higher value multipliers, and longer cooldowns.

📚
/skills — Permanent upgrades

Spend money on permanent skills that carry through prestige: Sharp Hooks (rare chance), Wide Nets (extra fish), Haggling (sale price), Treasure Sense (chest chance), Lockpicking (chest loot), Bait Thrift (bait saving), and Quick Cast (cooldown reduction). Each has multiple levels.

📈 Progression Progress

Level up, prestige, earn daily rewards, and track your lifetime stats across a deep progression system.

👤
/profile — Player card

View anyone's profile — level, prestige tier, clan, badges, active pet, and lifetime stats at a glance.

📊
/stats — Lifetime stats

Detailed fishing statistics: total casts, fish caught by rarity, money earned, biggest catch, and more.

🔥
/buffs — Active multipliers

See every buff affecting your casts — gear, skills, weather, boosts, clan buffs, rested tides, and event bonuses all in one view.

📝
/quests — Daily & weekly quests

Fresh objectives every day and week: catch X fish, earn Y money, dive to depth Z. Completing quests grants bonus money, XP, and sometimes Sea Crystals.

🎁
/daily — Daily reward

Claim a free reward once per day. Streaks increase the payout.

🎫
/license — Monthly reward track

A free 10-tier monthly reward track. Earn License XP from casts, daily claims, and quest completions. Tier 10 grants the Licensed badge. Resets every calendar month.

⭐
/prestige — Reset for power

At level 100, prestige to reset your progress in exchange for permanent buffs, a prestige badge (tiers I–X), and unlockable embed colors. Seasonal pets survive the reset. Up to 10 prestige tiers.

💎
/boosts — Temporary boosts & crew

Spend Sea Crystals on timed boosts — XP, money, rare fish, cooldown reduction — or hire a crew that fishes alongside you. Boosts stack duration (capped at 24 hours).

😴
Rested Tides

While you're offline, you accumulate rested charges (1 every 2 minutes, up to 200). Each charge grants +50% XP on your next cast, helping casual players keep pace.

🐾 Collections Progress

Chase rare drops, earn achievements, and build a collection that's uniquely yours.

🏅
/badge — Achievement badges

Earn badges for milestones — total casts, rare catches, prestige tiers, licensed months, and more. Displayed on your profile for all to see.

🔮
/relics — One-of-one collectibles

Relics are unique, procedurally generated collectibles that drop from any cast. Each has a tier (Common to Mythic), a material, an object, and an epithet — no two are alike. Inspect any relic to see its lore, precise drop rate, and lifetime cast number found on. Once caught, it's yours alone, forever.

👥 Social Social

OceanBite is more fun with friends — clans, co-op parties, community goals, leaderboards, and a referral program.

⚔️
/clan — Clans & raid bosses

Create or join a clan. Clan members share XP toward a clan level that grants passive buffs for everyone. Clan officers can summon raid bosses at chosen difficulties for big group payouts.

  • /clan create — Found a new clan
  • /clan join — Join by name
  • /clan info — View your clan
  • /clan raid — Summon a raid boss
  • /clan top — Clan leaderboard
  • /clan leave / /clan kick — Leave or remove a member
🏰
/clanhall — Clan Hall

Fund shared rooms together — each grants a clan-wide buff that grows with its level: Tackle Workshop (rare chance), Treasury (money), Scholars' Archive (XP), Galley Hall (fish weight), and Voyage Dock (exotic chance).

🎉
/party — Co-op fishing parties

Start or join a live fishing party in your server. A synergy buff scales with how many members are actively casting. A shared pot — minted from everyone's casts — splits equally at the end. Up to 5 parties can run simultaneously per server.

🤝
/community — Weekly server goal

Every server gets a weekly cooperative target (catch weight, money earned, etc.) that scales with active players. Hit the target and every qualifying contributor earns a reward pot, Sea Crystals, Harbor Tribute, and unique Galley recipes. Top contributors earn the Tidewarden's Seal.

📊
/top — Leaderboards

Server and global leaderboards for level, money, weight, casts, and more.

📍
/pos — Your ranks

See where you stand on every global leaderboard at a glance.

🪙
/coinflip — Gamble

Flip a coin to double your bet — or lose it. Simple, fast, high-stakes fun.

📨
/recruit — First Mate program

Share your invite link and earn milestone rewards when friends join and play. Recruiting also helps during World Boss fights.

🛠️ Server Setup Core

Server admins can configure OceanBite for their community with dedicated channels and a live activity feed.

📡
/activity enable — Set up OceanBite channels

Creates an OceanBite category with a start-fishing lobby and an always-on activity feed. The feed posts hourly per-angler digests and immediate alerts for purchases, voyages, milestones, relic drops, boss sweeps, and more.

  • /activity status — View current setup & settings
  • /activity teardown — Remove OceanBite channels
🧵
/start — Private fishing thread

Once a server is set up, each player gets their own private fishing thread — a clean, personal space to cast without cluttering public channels.

🌍 The World

A few systems run in the background, making every session feel different.

🌦️
Dynamic Weather

Weather rotates every 2 hours independently per biome. Rain, storms, fog, sunny spells, and more — each with gameplay effects on money, rare chance, XP, or cooldown.

📦
Treasure Chests

Random chests appear while fishing, containing money, XP, Sea Crystals, or exotic items. The Treasure Sense and Lockpicking skills improve your odds and payouts.

🛡️
Anti-Cheat

Periodic quick-reaction emoji checks ensure fair play. Just click the matching emoji when prompted — macros and self-bots are not tolerated.

📢
Activity Feed

Servers with OceanBite set up get a live activity channel: hourly fishing digests per player, plus instant alerts for big catches, relic drops, milestones, purchases, World Boss damage sweeps, and top-5 leaderboard shakeups.
